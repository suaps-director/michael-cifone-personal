Essays & Draft Papers for Workshop

The following essays are works in progress and are provided for discussion purposes only. Please do not cite without permission.

"Affect of the Impossible"

Summary: This paper argues that Unidentified Anomalous Phenomena (UAP) function as a "limit-experience" for our regimes of knowledge, acting as a powerful affective and political vector. By developing a new framework called "Existential Empiricism," the paper aims to provide a rational approach to the uncanny and the "excessive remainder" of UAP data that defies conventional explanation, thereby arguing for a new, holistic Wissenschaft capable of engaging with the "weird."
